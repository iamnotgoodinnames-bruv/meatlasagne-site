The Only Meat Lasagne
Recipe You Need

Rich bolognese, silky béchamel, hand-layered pasta. No shortcuts. No apologies.

25m
Prep
1h 20m
Cook
6
Serves
★ 10/10
Rating

Ingredients

  • 500g beef mince
  • 250g pork mince
  • 400g crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 4 garlic cloves
  • 1 carrot, grated
  • 150ml red wine
  • 12 lasagne sheets
  • 100g parmesan
  • 500ml béchamel
  • Fresh basil

Method

1
Brown the meat. Sauté onion and garlic until soft, add mince and cook until browned. Pour in red wine and reduce by half.
2
Build the bolognese. Add crushed tomatoes, paste, and carrot. Simmer on low for 45 minutes until rich and thick.
3
Layer it up. In a deep dish — bolognese, pasta sheet, béchamel, repeat. Finish with béchamel and a heavy hand of parmesan.
4
Bake. 180°C for 40 minutes covered, then 15 minutes uncovered until golden and bubbling. Rest 10 minutes before cutting.
